What's your typical response time?

I aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during weekdays. For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line and I'll prioritize your message.

Yes, I'm available for freelance and contract work. I can work on both short-term projects and long-term collaborations. Feel free to reach out with your project requirements.

My process typically includes: 1) Requirements gathering, 2) Planning & proposal, 3) Development & testing, 4) Deployment, and 5) Maintenance & support. I maintain clear communication throughout the project.

Absolutely! I work with clients from all around the world. My remote work setup allows me to collaborate effectively across different time zones using tools like Slack, Zoom, and project management platforms.

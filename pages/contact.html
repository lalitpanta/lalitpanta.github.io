Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in backend development (Python, Node.js, PHP), WordPress customization, blockchain applications, and full-stack web development. This includes RESTful APIs, database design, custom WordPress themes/plugins, smart contracts, and scalable web applications. I've worked on projects ranging from small business websites to complex enterprise systems.

What's your typical response time for inquiries?

I aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during weekdays. For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line and I'll prioritize your message. I also offer quick consultation calls for complex projects that require immediate discussion.

Do you offer freelance/contract work?

Yes, I'm available for freelance and contract work. I can work on both short-term projects and long-term collaborations. My typical engagement models include fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and retainer agreements for ongoing maintenance and support.

What's your development process like?

My process follows these key stages: 1) Discovery & requirements analysis, 2) Planning & proposal, 3) Design & prototyping, 4) Development & testing, 5) Deployment & launch, and 6) Maintenance & support. I maintain clear communication throughout the project with regular updates and progress reports.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I have experience working with clients from the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and across Europe. My remote work setup is optimized for international collaboration with tools like Slack, Zoom, GitHub, and project management platforms. I'm flexible with time zones and communication preferences.

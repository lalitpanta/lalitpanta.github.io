What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in backend development (Python, Node.js, PHP), WordPress customization, blockchain applications, and full-stack web development. This includes RESTful APIs, database design, custom WordPress themes/plugins, smart contracts, and scalable web applications.

What's your typical response time for inquiries?

I aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during weekdays. For urgent matters, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line and I'll prioritize your message.

Do you offer freelance/contract work?

Yes, I'm available for freelance and contract work. I can work on both short-term projects and long-term collaborations. My typical engagement models include fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and retainer agreements.

What's your development process like?

My process follows: 1) Discovery & requirements analysis, 2) Planning & proposal, 3) Design & prototyping, 4) Development & testing, 5) Deployment & launch, and 6) Maintenance & support.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I have experience working with clients from various countries. My remote work setup is optimized for international collaboration with tools like Slack, Zoom, GitHub, and project management platforms.
